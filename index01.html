About Me

Finance Professional with Purpose

Combining finance, compliance and technology to keep operations accurate and audit-ready.

I am a Finance & Compliance Professional based in London with a Master's degree in FinTech and Financial Markets, a Bachelor's degree in Finance, and I am currently pursuing the ACCA qualification.

Currently working at Harley Health Village, I support finance and compliance operations by managing financial reporting, supplier accounts, reconciliations, budgeting, payroll support, procurement, and audit preparation. My role focuses on ensuring financial accuracy, maintaining strong internal controls, and supporting regulatory compliance across the organisation.

I am passionate about combining finance, compliance, and technology to improve business processes, strengthen governance, and support informed decision-making. I am committed to continuous learning and professional development, with the goal of building a career that delivers value through accuracy, integrity, and operational excellence.
